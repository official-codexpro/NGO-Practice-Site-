About Swaniti Foundation

Swaniti is a non-profit that partners with governments and communities to strengthen systems and scale development solutions. We combine field experience, policy research and implementation support to improve service delivery and livelihoods across regions.

Policy Advisory

Evidence-backed briefs and strategic guidance for state and national decision-makers.

Implementation Support

Field-level pilots, capacity building and operational support to scale effective programs.

Research & Learning

Rigorous evaluations and reports to inform policy and practice.
